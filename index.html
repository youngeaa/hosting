What We Do

Accessibility Modifications
Done Right

Every installation is completed to ADA standards by trained professionals. We serve elderly and disabled individuals who qualify for the Louisiana Medicaid EAA waiver.

Wheelchair Ramps

Custom-built exterior and interior ramps with proper slope ratios, non-slip surfaces, and handrails — designed to meet ADA grade requirements for safe, independent entry and exit.

Grab Bars & Handrails

Properly anchored grab bars for bathrooms, hallways, and stairways — installed into wall studs for maximum load-bearing strength and client safety.

Widened Doorways

Structural doorway widening to a minimum 32–36 inch clear opening to accommodate wheelchairs and walkers — including hardware, trim, and full finish work.

Roll-In Showers & Tub Cuts

Convert standard tubs to walk-in access with step-down cuts, or install zero-threshold roll-in shower bases — fully waterproofed and finished for safe daily bathing.

Stair Lifts & Vertical Platform Lifts

Motorized stair lifts for straight or curved staircases, and vertical platform lifts for multi-level access — professionally installed and tested for reliable daily operation.
