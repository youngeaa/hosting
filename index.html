What We Do

Our Accessibility Services

Every modification is built with ADA accessibility standards in mind. We handle everything from assessment to final walkthrough — professionally and on schedule.

Wheelchair Ramps

Custom-built exterior and interior ramps designed to ADA slope requirements with non-slip surfaces and handrails for safe, independent access.

Exterior & interior ramps Non-slip surface materials Proper ADA slope ratio Includes handrails
Learn More

Grab Bars & Handrails

Structurally anchored grab bars installed into studs for maximum safety in bathrooms, hallways, and along stairways — built to hold when it matters most.

Stud-anchored installation Bathroom & shower bars Hallway & stairway rails Various finish options
Learn More

Widened Doorways

Full structural doorway widening to ADA-recommended 32–36 inch clear openings, accommodating wheelchairs and walkers with complete finish work included.

32–36" clear opening Structural modification Hardware & trim included Full finish work
Learn More

Roll-In Showers & Tub Cuts

Zero-threshold roll-in shower conversions and tub step-down cuts — fully waterproofed and tiled for safe, comfortable daily bathing without obstacles.

Zero-threshold entry Tub cut-down conversion Fully waterproofed Professional tile finish
