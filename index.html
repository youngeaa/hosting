What We Do

Our Accessibility Services

Every modification is installed to ADA standards. We handle everything — permits, coordination with your case manager, and professional installation.

Wheelchair Ramps

Custom-built exterior and interior ramps designed to ADA slope requirements with non-slip surfaces and handrails for safe, independent access.

Exterior & interior ramps Non-slip surface materials Proper ADA slope ratio Includes handrails

Grab Bars & Handrails

Structurally anchored grab bars installed into studs for maximum safety in bathrooms, hallways, and along stairways — built to hold when it matters most.

Stud-anchored installation Bathroom & shower bars Hallway & stairway rails Various finish options

Widened Doorways

Full structural doorway widening to ADA-compliant 32–36 inch clear openings — accommodating wheelchairs and walkers with complete finish work included.

32–36" clear opening Structural modification Hardware & trim included Full finish work

Roll-In Showers & Tub Cuts

Zero-threshold roll-in shower conversions and tub step-down cuts — fully waterproofed and tiled for safe, comfortable daily bathing without obstacles.

Zero-threshold entry Tub cut-down conversion Fully waterproofed Professional tile finish

Stair Lifts & Platform Lifts

Motorized stair lifts for straight staircases and vertical platform lifts for multi-level access — professionally installed, tested, and ready for daily use.

Straight stair lifts Vertical platform lifts Professional installation Fully tested on completion

Medicaid EAA Program

We Handle the Paperwork — You Get the Modifications

As a state-approved Environmental Accessibility Adaptation (EAA) contractor, we work directly with your Medicaid case manager to authorize and complete your home modifications at no out-of-pocket cost to eligible participants.

1

Confirm Eligibility

We verify your Medicaid EAA waiver status with your case manager before any work begins.

2

Assessment & Authorization

We assess your home and submit the required documentation for state approval.

3

Approved & Scheduled

Once authorized, we schedule your installation at your convenience — no delays.

4

Professional Installation

All work is completed to code. We bill Medicaid directly — you pay nothing.
